Oreogasm Ice Cream

INGREDIENTS :

  • 3 c. heavy cream
  • 1 14-oz. can sweetened condensed milk
  • crushed Oreos (about 30 Oreos), plus more for topping
  • 1/4 c. caramel, plus more for toppin
  • 1/4 c. chocolate sauce, plus more for topping

METHOD :

  • In a stand mixer fitted with the whisk attachment, beat cream until stiff peaks form.
  • Fold in sweetened condensed milk and Oreos until fully combined, then drizzle in caramel and chocolate sauce and stir gently until just combined.

  • Let soften 10 minutes before serving.
